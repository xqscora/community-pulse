Okay, wow. That’s… awful. My stomach just dropped a little. It’s the library, isn’t it? I practically live there. It feels so sudden, like someone just ripped out a part of, well, everything. It’s a really sharp, immediate feeling of…loss, I think. Not a huge, overwhelming panic, but a definite tightening.

Honestly, the biggest thing pulling at me is the *uncertainty*. It’s not just that the library’s closing, it's the way it was announced – so abrupt. The idea of all those books, those quiet spaces, just…gone. My biggest worry is that it'll make it even harder to find resources for my internship applications. The robotics internship specifically mentioned needing a strong research background, and the library is where I’ve been building that. 

But, I guess, I also hope that maybe, *just maybe*, the town council will reconsider. There has to be some way to keep the core services running, even if it’s just a scaled-back version. It feels like a really important place, and it's worth pushing for. 

Right now, I’m going to email the town councilors – the ones I know from the school board meetings – and ask if there's any information about the budget cuts and if there’s a chance to voice support for keeping the library open.
