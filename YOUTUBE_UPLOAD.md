## Description
```
Community Pulse is a 10-resident policy simulator built for Gemma 4 + Ollama. Drop a policy ("close the library", "add a clinic", "internet outage for a week") and watch ten agent archetypes — each backed by a four-layer neurobiological personality engine (Mini-Cerome) — react in character, talk to each other, and shift a small social graph. The output is a 3- or 7-day trajectory of mean stress, tie trust, and resilience — a pre-flight check before real consultation, runnable entirely on a laptop with no data leaving the device.

Submitted to the Gemma 4 Good Hackathon 2026.
```
